Allows you to change the word "cart" to a word or phrase of your choice throughout the Woocommerce frontend.

== Description ==
This plugin adds a setting to Woocommerce > Settings > General Options where you can enter a new word or phrase to use instead of the word "cart" throughout Wordpress. The setting is labelled Replace the word "Cart".

1. The setting is found under Woocommerce > Settings > General Options
